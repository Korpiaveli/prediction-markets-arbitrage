### Parlay Arbitrage Strategies

#### What is Parlay Arbitrage?
Exploiting odds differences across sportsbooks for multi-leg bets.
Requires:
1. Multiple sportsbook APIs
2. Rapid odds comparison
3. Correlated outcome detection

#### Implementation Considerations
- **Complexity:** Higher than single-market arbitrage
- **Liquidity:** May be limited for obscure combinations
- **Regulatory:** State-by-state sports betting laws
- **Timeline:** Phase 2+ after core system stable

#### Potential Parlay Arbitrage Pairs
1. **Betfair ‚Üî Smarkets** (UK/EU)
2. **US Sportsbooks** (state-dependent licensing)
3. **Crypto Sportsbooks ‚Üî Traditional** (Azuro, etc.)
